About Stargzr

Stargzr is a network of curious minds who dream, learn, teach, and collaborate beyond the classroom. We believe that education should not be confined to textbooks and exams, but should be a journey of exploration and discovery.

Born from a simple idea that students learn best when they're genuinely curious and engaged, Stargzr has grown into a community where knowledge flows freely, where teaching is peer-to-peer, and where projects are driven by passion rather than grades.

We are inspired by those who "stargaze" — those who look beyond what is immediately visible, who dream big, and who refuse to accept limitations. Whether you're interested in physics, programming, philosophy, or poetry, if you have curiosity and the desire to learn and share, you belong here.

Founder's Note

Stargzr started as a small astronomy club in Bogura, where a few of us would gather to talk about the stars, the universe, and everything in between. What began as casual stargazing sessions evolved into something much bigger — a movement of students who wanted to learn on their own terms.

Today, Stargzr is more than just astronomy. It's a philosophy: the belief that curiosity should drive education, that knowledge should be accessible to all, and that the best teachers are often our peers. I invite you to join us on this journey of exploration and discovery.

— Himel Das, Founder

Our Philosophy

Curiosity: We believe that genuine curiosity is the foundation of all learning.

Exploration: Education is not about memorizing facts, but about exploring ideas and discovering new perspectives.

Collaboration: The best learning happens when we work together, share knowledge, and teach each other.

Independence: We empower members to learn independently and pursue their own interests.

Rebellion: We challenge conventional education systems and create new ways of learning.
